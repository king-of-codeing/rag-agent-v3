\# Acme IT Department — Frequently Asked Questions



\## Password Management



\### How do I reset my password?

Visit the self-service portal at https://passwords.acme-example.com.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, contain one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.

Passwords expire every 90 days.



\### What if I forgot my security questions?

Contact the IT help desk at extension 4357 (HELP) for manual verification and password reset.



\### Why can't I reuse old passwords?

The system blocks reuse of your last 10 passwords for security compliance.



\## VPN Access



\### How do I connect to the corporate VPN?

1\. Install the Acme VPN client from the IT Software Center.

2\. Launch the client and sign in with your corporate credentials.

3\. Select your nearest gateway (US-East, EU-West, or APAC).

4\. Click Connect. A green icon in your system tray confirms the connection.



\### Is VPN required to work from home?

Yes. All access to internal systems (HR portal, code repositories, internal wikis) requires an active VPN connection.



\### Why does my VPN disconnect frequently?

This usually means weak WiFi or ISP throttling. Try switching to a wired connection or contacting IT.



\## Hardware Requests



\### How do I request a new laptop?

Submit a hardware request via the IT Service Catalog. Standard laptops are issued within 5 business days.

Requests for non-standard hardware (e.g., MacBook Pro, dual-monitor setups) require manager approval.



\### What is the laptop refresh cycle?

Acme refreshes employee laptops every 3 years. Eligible employees receive an email notification 30 days before their refresh date.



\### Can I keep my old laptop after refresh?

No. All retired hardware is securely wiped and returned to inventory or recycled.



\## Software Installation



\### Can I install personal software on my work laptop?

No. Only software approved by IT and listed in the Software Center may be installed.

Unauthorized software may trigger a security alert and result in temporary device lockout.



\### How do I request new software?

Submit a Software Request Form on the IT portal with a business justification. Most requests are reviewed within 3 business days.



\### Are there approved alternatives to common tools?

Yes. The IT portal lists pre-approved alternatives: e.g., Notion for documentation, Slack for chat, GitHub for code.
