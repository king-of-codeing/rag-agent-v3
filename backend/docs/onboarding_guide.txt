ACME CORPORATION — NEW HIRE ONBOARDING GUIDE
Welcome to Acme!

DAY 1 CHECKLIST
1. Pick up your laptop from IT (Building B, Floor 3, Room 314).
2. Set up your password at the self-service portal.
3. Install the corporate VPN client from the IT Software Center.
4. Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
5. Complete the security awareness training (60 min, mandatory).

WEEK 1 CHECKLIST
- Meet your manager and team
- Review the Employee Policy Handbook (covers leave, WFH, expenses)
- Read the Information Security Handbook
- Set up your development environment (engineers only — see API docs)
- Schedule a 1:1 with your buddy

FIRST 30 DAYS
- Complete role-specific training modules in the LMS
- Shadow a senior team member for at least 5 hours
- Set your first quarterly OKR goals with your manager
- Familiarize yourself with the Acme Platform API if applicable

KEY CONTACTS
- IT Help Desk: extension 4357 (HELP)
- HR: extension 4472 (HIRE)
- Security incidents: security@acme-example.com or extension SEC-911
- Facilities: extension 3325 (FACE)

BENEFITS HIGHLIGHTS
- 24 days paid annual leave
- 12 days sick leave  
- Comprehensive health insurance from day 1
- 401(k) match up to 5%
- $1,500 annual learning budget
- WFH up to 3 days per week (with manager approval)

YOUR FIRST QUESTIONS
Ask your manager, your buddy, or the HR team. There are no dumb questions in week 1.
Welcome aboard!
