ACME CORPORATION - EMPLOYEE POLICY HANDBOOK
Version 2026.1

SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY
Page 1
All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave must be requested at least 7 days in advance through the HR portal.
Unused leave (up to 5 days) can be carried over to the next calendar year.
Sick leave is granted for up to 12 days per year and requires a medical certificate beyond 2 consecutive days.

SECTION 2: WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Page 2
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Remote workers must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (local time zone).
Company-issued laptops must be used for all work; personal devices are not permitted for accessing internal systems.

SECTION 3: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Page 3
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence.
Receipts are mandatory for all expenses above $25.
Approved categories include travel, client meals, office supplies, and professional development courses up to $1,500 per year.

SECTION 4: CODE OF CONDUCT
Page 4
All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct in the workplace.
Harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR within 5 business days of arising.
