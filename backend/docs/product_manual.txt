SECTION 1: GETTING STARTED
Page 1
Welcome to the Acme SmartHub X1. Before first use, charge the device for at least 4 hours using the included USB-C cable.
The status LED will turn solid green when charging is complete.
Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds to turn the device on.

SECTION 2: WIFI SETUP
Page 2
The SmartHub X1 supports both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz WiFi networks.
To connect: open the Acme Home app, tap "Add Device", and follow the on-screen prompts.
If pairing fails, perform a factory reset by holding the reset button for 10 seconds until the LED blinks red.

SECTION 3: TROUBLESHOOTING
Page 3
Problem: Device won't power on.
Solution: Ensure the battery is charged. If the LED does not light up during charging, contact support.

Problem: WiFi connection drops frequently.
Solution: Move the device closer to your router. Walls and metal objects can weaken the signal.

Problem: App cannot find the device.
Solution: Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone and you are within 10 meters of the SmartHub.

SECTION 4: WARRANTY
Page 4
The SmartHub X1 comes with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, water exposure, or unauthorized repairs.
To file a warranty claim, contact support@acme-example.com with your serial number and proof of purchase.
